Insia Fatima

by She Made It | Mar 20, 2023 | Founder | CEO She Made It

Insia Fatima Larik, a trailblazing female engineer, embarked on a remarkable journey that led her to become the esteemed founder and CEO of She Made It. As the first female engineer in her family, Insia's achievement symbolizes her unwavering determination to break barriers and excel in a field traditionally dominated by men.

With a strong technical background in computer systems engineering, Insia's expertise and passion for technology became the driving force behind her mission. However, her aspirations extended far beyond personal success. She aimed to empower women and create a platform where their talents could shine. From a young age, Insia demonstrated her leadership abilities. At just 17 years old, she fearlessly hosted a radio show in a village, using her platform to amplify the voices of women and advocate for their empowerment. This experience laid the foundation for her commitment to uplifting women and providing them with opportunities to express themselves.

Insia Fatima Larik's journey is a remarkable testament to the transformative power of determination, passion, and entrepreneurship. As she leads She Made It into the future, her commitment to empowering women and fostering economic independence remains the driving force behind her success.
